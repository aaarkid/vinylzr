About Vinylzr

Vinylzr is a platform that allows vinyl enthusiasts to track their vinyl plays, creating a personalized record of their listening habits. Our goal is to help you organize your collection and enhance your vinyl experience.

We hope you enjoy using Vinylzr and find it as useful and engaging as we do. Keep spinning those records!
